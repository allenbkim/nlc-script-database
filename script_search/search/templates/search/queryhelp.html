Search Query Help

The Norman Lear Center Script Database site allows for the use of standard boolean logic such as AND (&), OR (|), and NOT (!).

To find scripts containing variations of the word "donation" but not variations of "politics" nor "organ":
donation & !politics & !organ

Parentheses can be used to group items together. E.g., if one word is required and one other word within a group is required:
drive & (blood | food)

A special operator "<->" creates searches for words appearing next to each other:
blood<->drive | food<->drive

The "appearing next to" operator can be slightly modified to find words n spots from each other:
habitat<2>humanity | make<3>foundation
